❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the bot free to use?

Yes! The bot is completely free and open-source. You can use it, modify it, and contribute to it.

Is my laptop secure when using this bot?

Yes! Only authorized users (configured in .env) can execute commands. Your bot token is kept private and never shared. However, always be cautious about who you authorize.

Can I modify the bot's commands?

Absolutely! The bot is open-source, so you can modify any command or add new features. Check the codebase in the commands/ directory.

What operating systems are supported?

The bot supports Windows, macOS, and Linux. Some features like Chrome detection are optimized for Windows, but most commands work on all platforms.

How often is the bot updated?

The bot is actively maintained! Updates and new features are added regularly. Follow the GitHub repository to stay updated with the latest releases.

Do I need special permissions on my Discord server?

The bot needs Administrator permissions to function fully. When you invite the bot to your server, it will request these permissions. You can also set specific channel permissions if desired.
